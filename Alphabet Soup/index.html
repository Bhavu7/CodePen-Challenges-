CLASSIC ALPHABET SOUP ingredients 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 large onion diced 2 carrots diced 2 celery stalks diced 3 cloves garlic minced 8 cups chicken broth 1 can 14.5 oz diced tomatoes 1 cup alphabet pasta 2 cups cooked chicken shredded 1 teaspoon dried oregano 1 teaspoon dried basil salt and pepper to taste 2 tablespoons fresh parsley chopped INSTRUCTIONS heat olive oil in large pot over medium heat add onion carrots celery cook 5 minutes until softened add garlic cook 1 minute more add chicken broth diced tomatoes oregano basil bring to boil add alphabet pasta cook 8-10 minutes until tender add cooked chicken season with salt pepper simmer 5 minutes stir in fresh parsley serve hot enjoy your homemade alphabet soup perfect for cold days warming and satisfying serves 6 people prep time 15 minutes cook time 25 minutes total time 40 minutes
